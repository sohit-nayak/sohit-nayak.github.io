Optimization of EV Charging Stations in WA

Project information

  • Category: Optimization
  • Project date: 22 November, 2022
  • Project URL: Github Repository

Mass-market EVs have been available in the U.S. for more than a decade, but there have only been small, incremental changes in electric car sales for most of that time. However, in the first three months of 2022, EV registrations shot up a huge 60 percent, according to a report by Automotive News. Hence, a natural consequence of this would be the planning and setting of the Electric Charging ecosystems. This project attempts to solve this problem for the state of Washington, where we try to find out the optimal locations of EV Charging Stations in every county.
